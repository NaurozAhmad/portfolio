About This Project

This is the one of the projects of WWF-P. WWF-P started a project in sulimania(pakistan) of chilghoza, where the chilghoza are produce in large number.now-a-days the forest of chilghoza are almost and chilghoza production is decreasing day by day .Chilghozas are necessary for a good health, it contains carbohydrates and proteins.
We have hard working staff who are capable of promoting the importance of Chalghozas among people and making them aware about it's benefits. The purpose of this website is to give information to the people about chilghozas & marketing of Chilghozas. We deal best quality Chalghozas and making it available in almost every market of any city.

One of the largest stands in the World, Chilghoza Forests of Suleiman Range is spaced over 260km2. These forests play an important role both as ecological and environmental services besides providing a credible source of income to local communities. Due to increase in population coupled with lack of alternate livelihood opportunities, Chilghoza Forests are under constant pressure for timber.

WWF-P has established two processing units in the area to produce quality nuts and encourage the local communities to get fully involved in the entire process. The local people have also been trained in pre-and-post harvest techniques of Chilghoza nuts. This will lead to increased incomes that in turn will help in the conservation of Chilghoza forest ecosystem of Suleiman Range

 

Mission & Objectives

Our Mission:

  • Improving  livelihood opportunities of the surrounding dwellers of Chilghoza forest of Suleiman Range
  • Promoting marketing of Chilghoza nuts
  • Build local capacity for sustainable development and conservation of biodiversity

 

Our Ojectives

  • Mobilize the local communities and organize their formal groups (CBOs) for improved marketing of Chilghoza Nuts and conservation of Chilghoza Forests.
  • Promote the use of the internet among the locals for market information and trading of Chilghoza nuts
  • Support the local communities in proper marketing of Chilghoza nuts in the international market
  • Certification of the product (fair trade/ organic certification)
